ABOUT US

Welcome to eMedicare

eMedicare is an online platform which provides its user a better option to diagnose their health problems by entering their symptoms. Effective medical care is now just a few clicks away.

  • Quick Assistance
  • Symptoms
  • Results
  • Consultation
  • Action Plan

In doubt of your current health? Feeling a bit hazy or lethargic? Having a headache or a joint pain is not allowing you to move? Want a quick checkup to figure out what could be the reason? No worries, just follow few easy steps and and get a quick online report.

Enter your symptoms and fill in other details as required by our system to search for the disease related to your symptoms. For example: If you are suffereing from a headache, then select headache and proceed similarly.

Our system will generate the results based on your symptoms. The result will be the disease from which you may be suffering or are prone to, if proper prevention measures are not taken.

Our system will also suggest and consult doctors as per the symptoms you enter. The doctors are consulted on the basis of your location and aslo on the basis of their field of expertise.

It is advisable to visit a doctor if any chronic disease is detected and chalk out an action plan to get healthy as soon as possible.
