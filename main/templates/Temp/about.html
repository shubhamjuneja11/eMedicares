ABOUT US

In today’s world we still have to manually fill out those long checklists at every doctor’s office. We have no access to our own health data. And we often end up with non-differentiated treatments instead of individualized ones that make us better sooner.

Yes we can change this. The first step is realizing that the vision of home-health is science – not fiction.

eMedicare is an online platform which aims to provide its users better options for early detection of a disease and subsequent care. It is a website especially designed for people who are facing problems related to their health. The website tries to inform a person about the multiple diseases he or she may have depending upon his or her symptoms.

As the old adage goes “Prevention is better than cure”. eMedicare provides free access to comprehensive and reliable health care services and covers all aspects of pro-preventive care too. We strongly feel that the health of individuals play a pivotal role in a country’s development. The health of populations is a distinct key issue in public policy discourse in every mature society often determining the progress of that huge society. In a diverse country like India which is plagued by economic disparity, it is the need of the hour to give special attention to vulnerable groups such as children, women, disabled and the aged.We are constantly working to provide quality services to each section of the society. We believe that expanding the use of ‘mobile’ health solutions could in itself generate billions of rupees in savings and improve outcomes. eMedicare will be the first triage step. If data can be gathered at home, then a diagnosis from a remote doctor’s office becomes a much more realistic alternative. And that means more patients can self-administer follow-up routine care at home.We, in our backyard, are working with grit and gumption to improve the experience of health care services in India as it is the fundamental right of each every citizen to receive proper medical services.

Mission Statement

To remain the epicentre of healthcare and provide an unfailing service to all, to uphold our leadership in healthcare technology, and to further strengthen our prominence in satisfying the healthcare needs of diverse populations around the globe. eMedicare will forever remain dedicated to providing quality and easily accessible technological solutions with an undying attention to clinical detail and excellence, an unparalleled commitment towards patient safety and universally accepted solutions.
